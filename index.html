Why Protein is Non-Negotiable
During a deficit, protein (1.8–2.4 g/kg) is the primary muscle-preservation lever (Morton et al., 2018 — 49-study meta-analysis). Thermic effect: 25–30% of calories burned just digesting it.

Training vs Rest Day Calories
Training (6 days): Full calorie target. Carbs fuel glycogen and performance.

Rest day (1 day): Reduce carbs by 150–200 kcal. Deepens weekly deficit without harming recovery.
